Understanding the Impact of Obesity on Orthopaedic Health: Insights by Dr. Srikanth Varma



Introduction: Obesity poses a significant threat to overall health, with orthopaedic problems being among its serious consequences. Dr. Srikanth Varma sheds light on the correlation between obesity and various orthopaedic conditions, emphasizing the importance of weight management in alleviating symptoms and improving quality of life.

1. Osteoarthritis and Obesity: A Troublesome Connection Osteoarthritis, a debilitating condition affecting joints, is exacerbated by obesity. Dr. Srikanth Varma explains how excessive weight burdens weight-bearing joints like the knees, hips, and hands, accelerating the degenerative process and causing inflammation.

2. Osteoporosis and Bone Health Obesity may accelerate bone loss, leading to osteoporosis, a condition characterized by reduced bone density and increased fracture risk. Dr. Varma highlights the importance of muscle mass in maintaining bone strength, emphasizing the role of physical activity in mitigating bone loss.

3. Low Back Pain: A Common Consequence of Obesity Low back pain, often stemming from degenerative disc disease, is strongly associated with obesity. Dr. Srikanth Varma discusses how excess weight contributes to spinal stress and disc degeneration, leading to debilitating back pain.

4. Other Orthopaedic Conditions Linked to Obesity Dr. Varma elucidates how obesity increases the risk of various orthopaedic ailments, including tendonitis, nerve compressions, plantar fasciitis, trochanteric bursitis, gout, and fibromyalgia. He emphasizes the importance of weight management in preventing and managing these conditions.

5. Surgical Interventions and Weight Management In cases where orthopaedic conditions necessitate surgical intervention, weight management plays a crucial role in optimizing outcomes. Dr. Srikanth Varma discusses surgical options like knee osteotomy for addressing osteoarthritis, highlighting the importance of weight reduction in enhancing surgical success.

Conclusion: Dr. Srikanth Varma underscores the intricate relationship between obesity and orthopaedic health, emphasizing the need for proactive weight management to alleviate symptoms and prevent complications. As a distinguished Orthopaedic Surgeon in Al Nahda, Dubai, Dr. Varma remains committed to delivering comprehensive orthopaedic care, guiding patients towards healthier lifestyles and improved orthopaedic outcomes.
